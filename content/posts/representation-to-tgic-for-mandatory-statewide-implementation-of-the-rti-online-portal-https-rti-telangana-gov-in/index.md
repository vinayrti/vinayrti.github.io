**Subject:
Representation regarding Mandatory Statewide Implementation of the RTI
Online Portal (<https://rti.telangana.gov.in/>)
in Compliance with the RTI Act, 2005 and Judicial Directions.**

Respected Sir,

I, B. Vinay
Reddy, a citizen and frequent user of the Right to Information mechanism in
Telangana, respectfully submit this representation seeking your kind
intervention and leadership in ensuring mandatory, uniform, and meaningful
implementation of the Telangana RTI Online Portal (<https://rti.telangana.gov.in/>)
across all public authorities in the State, including TGIC itself, in
accordance with the RTI Act, 2005 and binding directions of the Hon’ble Supreme
Court and Hon’ble High Court of Telangana.

**Appreciation for Enabling
Online Filing of Second Appeals & Complaints**

At the outset, I
convey my sincere gratitude to your good self for operationalising the facility
for online filing of Second Appeals and Complaints on the TGIC website.

I had earlier
submitted a representation on 12-11-2025 regarding this matter, and I
deeply appreciate the Commission’s timely initiative. This reform reflects
TGIC’s commitment to transparency, digital governance, and citizen convenience.
