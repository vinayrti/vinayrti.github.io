## Why Is There a Delay?

Due to the **huge number of RTI applications** being filed for certified copies, ICAI is finding it difficult to strictly adhere to the statutory timeline.

Additionally, ICAI is carrying out **suo motu verification of marks** before supplying copies ‚Äî a process done **in the interest of students**, but which consumes additional time.

Because of this:
- There is **no fixed number of days**
- On average, students are receiving copies within **30‚Äì40 days**
- Delays beyond 30 days are occurring in genuine cases

However, you **will receive your copies** ‚Äî there is no need to panic.
